[TÜBİTAK 2209-A Grant Funded](https://tubitak.gov.tr/en/scholarships/lisans-onlisans/destek-programlari/2209-research-project-support-programme-undergraduate-students)

* Jul 2025-Oct 2025: Undergraduate Researcher

  * Validated the cell plasticity signature in TCGA-LIHC by Cox regression analysis, linking it to drug resistance, relapse, and survival.
  * Identified distinct non-genetic phenotypic states in clonal Huh7 cell populations using scRNA-seq. [Erasmus+ Funded Traineeship](https://erasmus-plus.ec.europa.eu/opportunities/opportunities-for-individuals/students/traineeships-abroad-for-students)

  * Assisted with lentiviral transduction, cell culture passaging, mouse xenograft modeling, and flow cytometry.

* May 2023-Jul 2024: Undergraduate Researcher & Intern

  * Comprehensive Analysis of GPRC5A Promoter Methylation Across Cancer Types
  * Methly-seq pilot study [poster](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/374535049_Comprehensive_Analysis_of_Promoter_Methylation_Effects_on_the_GPRC5A_Methylation_Profile_Across_Various_Cancer_Types) presented at a [bioinformatics symposium](https://hibit2023.hacettepe.edu.tr/).
